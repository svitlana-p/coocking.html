Choco Cake

Recipe:

  • Flour 250 g
  • Butter 60 g
  • Olive oil 60 g
  • Vanilla extract 2 teaspoons
  • Milk 280 g
  • Wine vinegar 1 tablespoon

    • Mix all dry ingredients, add all wet ingredients, mix ones more. Bake at 175 degrees for 1 hour.

Fondant:

  1. Cocoa 100 g
  2. Milk 100 ml (maybe more depends on cocoa)
  3. Sugar 35 g
  4. Oil 1 teaspoon

    5. Mix all ingredients, cook over low heat for 10 minutes. Put on top of the finished cake. Bon appetit!
